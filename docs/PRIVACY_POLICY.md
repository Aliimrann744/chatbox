# Privacy Policy — Whatchat

_Last updated: April 24, 2026_

Whatchat ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains
what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Information you give us
- **Account details**: name, phone number, email address, profile photo (optional),
  about/bio text.
- **Communications**: messages, voice notes, photos, videos, documents, locations,
  and contacts you share via the app. Messages are stored only so that they can be
  delivered and kept in sync across your devices.
- **Authentication data**: one-time passcodes (OTPs), two-factor authentication
  secrets and backup codes, device-login events.

### 1.2 Information we collect automatically
- **Device identifiers**: a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to send
  push notifications and calls.
- **Usage and diagnostic data**: crash reports, error logs, session timestamps,
  app version and OS version. Used only to diagnose and improve the service.
- **Contacts (optional)**: if you grant permission, we upload phone numbers from
  your address book so we can show you which contacts also use Whatchat.
- **Media access (optional)**: camera, microphone, and photo library access are
  used only when you choose to send media or make a call.
- **Location (optional)**: used only when you explicitly share a location in a chat.

### 1.3 Information we do **not** collect
- We do not sell your data.
- We do not display advertising.
- We do not read the content of your chats beyond what is required to store and
  deliver them.

## 2. How we use your information

- Deliver and sync your chats, calls, and status updates.
- Authenticate you via OTP, Google Sign-In, or Facebook Login.
- Send you push notifications for new messages and incoming calls.
- Detect and prevent abuse, spam, and fraud.
- Improve the app using anonymized diagnostics and crash reports.
- Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Sharing

We do not share your data with third parties for their own marketing. We share
only with service providers who help us run Whatchat, under contractual
confidentiality:
- Google (Firebase Cloud Messaging — push notifications).
- Google / Facebook (only the OAuth tokens you sign in with, for identity
  verification).
- Our cloud storage provider (media files you upload in chats).

We may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of
our users.

## 4. Data retention

- Messages and media remain stored as long as you keep your account.
- Account data is deleted within **30 days** of account deletion.
- Diagnostic logs are retained for up to **90 days** and then purged.

## 5. Your rights

You can at any time, from inside the app:
- **Access**: Settings → Account → Request my data. We email you a downloadable
  archive within a few days.
- **Correct**: edit your profile details in Settings.
- **Delete**: Settings → Account → Delete account. Deletion is permanent and
  removes your messages from our servers.
- **Deactivate**: pause your account without deleting messages.

If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, you have additional rights under the GDPR (erasure,
portability, objection, lodging a complaint with your data protection authority).

## 6. Children

Whatchat is not directed at children under 13 (under 16 in the EU). We do not
knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an
account, contact us and we will remove it.

## 7. Security

- All network traffic is encrypted with TLS 1.2+.
- Access tokens are stored in the device's secure keystore (Android Keystore /
  iOS Keychain).
- Two-factor authentication is available in Settings → Account.
- You can review active sessions in Settings → Account → Login events.

## 8. International transfers

We operate servers in the United States and Europe. By using Whatchat you consent
to your data being processed in these regions, with safeguards as required by
applicable law.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We'll post any changes here and update the "Last updated" date. Material changes
will also be announced in-app.
